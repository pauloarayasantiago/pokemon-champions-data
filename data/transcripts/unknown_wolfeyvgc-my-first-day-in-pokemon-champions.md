- **Performance**: Switch (non-Switch 2) has notable frame rate lag — Switch 2 version needed
- **Pokemon Home integration**: Works smoothly; importing Pokemon from Home is the recommended way to get your roster quickly
- **Tutorial**: Has at least one soft lock bug (fixed by reconnecting via hotspot)
- **Early team observations**: Incineroar and Torkoal appear on WolfeyVGC's very first teams — consistent with their later high usage rates
- **Torkoal + Sun**: Early use of Torkoal with Victreebel suggests Sun core viability was recognized on day 1
- **Item shop**: Held items are present and purchasable with VP; Flame Orb and other S/V staples confirmed absent at launch
